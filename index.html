Philosophy

  1. Do great work with great people: Work on projects worth working on with teams worth working for. Organizations are design documents.
  2. Document and share everything: Sharing documentation says you believe everyone has something to contribute and gives people the knowledge to do so.
  3. Small teams get stuff done: The best products happen when multi-disciplinary teams are empowered and trusted to work together. The most important work comes from the bottom.
  4. Ship fast, and iterate faster: Shipping is the beginning. The next steps are the most important, when we take the time to listen, learn, and improve.
  5. Take care of each other, and yourself: Nothing happens without us. If we‚Äôre not healthy we can‚Äôt help each other. We burn out alone or we spark together.
