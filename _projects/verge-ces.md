The Verge wanted to rethink its coverage of the Consumer Electronics Show (CES) using emerging technologies. After brainstorming with the editorial team, we came up with a couple ideas. First we should showcase the best conversations at CES, which we believed was happening in The Verge Slack, so I helped hook up the chat room to a live web based app (with copy editing protections).
